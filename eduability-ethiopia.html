♿ Why EduAbility

Built for Every Student

A platform designed from the ground up to empower Ethiopian students with disabilities — combining accessibility, local culture, and smart technology.

👐

Sign Language Support

Ethiopian Sign Language (ESL) lessons for deaf students, with animated avatar demonstrations and an interactive ESL dictionary for all subjects.

🔊

Audio Learning for Blind Students

Every lesson is available in high-quality audio narration in Amharic, Afaan Oromo, Tigrinya, and English. Full screen reader and braille display support.

🤖

Personalized AI Tutor

An intelligent tutor that adapts to each student's pace, explains concepts simply, generates quizzes, and gives feedback in their preferred language.

📚

Ethiopian Curriculum — Grade 1–12

All subjects aligned with the Ethiopian Ministry of Education curriculum, including textbook PDFs, interactive lessons, and national exam preparation.

📶

Offline Mode for Rural Areas

Download lessons and textbooks for offline use — critical for students in rural Ethiopia with limited internet access. Low-bandwidth mode available.

🗣️

Speech-to-Text Input

Students who cannot type can speak their answers and questions. Supports Amharic and English voice input for all assignments and AI tutor interactions.

📅

Ethiopian Calendar Integration

Fully integrated Ethiopian calendar (Ge'ez calendar) with Ethiopian time format support and alignment to the Ethiopian school year and holidays.

🏆

Gamified Learning

Earn badges, points, and certificates as you learn. Inclusive game mechanics designed so all students — regardless of disability — can participate and excel.

👨‍🏫

Teacher & Parent Dashboards

Teachers can assign lessons, track student progress, and communicate with parents. Parents can monitor their child's learning journey in real time.
